About Me

I am a Health Data Science graduate student at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF), with research experience spanning biomedical AI, statistical modeling, medical imaging, and translational data science. My current work focuses on cancer risk modeling and machine learning applications in healthcare. More broadly, I am interested in computational biology, precision medicine, and developing data-driven methods to better understand disease mechanisms and improve clinical decision-making.
